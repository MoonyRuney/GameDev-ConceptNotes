Element Types

Each Element Type has its own strengths and weaknesses against other Element Types, creating a dynamic system of interactions that can influence combat strategies, puzzle-solving, and environmental navigation. There are 7 Element Types: Aqua, Ignis, Terra, Aero, Lumen, Umbra and Vis.

Element Type Interactions

  • Aqua (Water) is strong against Ignis (Fire) but weak to Aero (Air).
  • Ignis (Fire) is strong against Terra (Earth) but weak to Aqua (Water).
  • Terra (Earth) is strong against Aero (Air) but weak to Ignis (Fire).
  • Aero (Air) is strong against Aqua (Water) but weak to Terra (Earth).
  • Lumen (Light) is strong against Umbra (Darkness) but weak to Umbra (Darkness).
  • Umbra (Darkness) is strong against Lumen (Light) but weak to Lumen (Light).
  • Vis (Neutral/Normal) is the balance of all elements, and the absence of any element. It is weak against no elements, but also not strong against any elements.
