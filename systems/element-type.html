Element Types

Each Element Type has its own strengths and weaknesses against other Element Types, creating a dynamic system of interactions that can influence combat strategies, puzzle-solving, and environmental navigation. There are 7 Element Types: Aqua, Ignis, Terra, Aero, Lumen, Umbra and Vis.

Element Type Interactions

  • Aqua is strong against Ignis but weak to Aero.
  • Ignis is strong against Terra but weak to Aqua.
  • Terra is strong against Aero but weak to Ignis.
  • Aero is strong against Aqua but weak to Terra.
  • Lumen is strong against Umbra but weak to Umbra.
  • Umbra is strong against Lumen but weak to Lumen.
  • Vis is the balance of all elements, and the absence of any element. It is weak against no elements, but also not strong against any elements.
